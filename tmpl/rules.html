RULES

Objective:


The Hangman web game is an online adaptation of the classic word-guessing game. Your goal is to guess the hidden word before the hangman is fully drawn. Each incorrect guess brings the hangman one step closer, so choose your letters wisely!"


How to Play:


Guessing a letter:

  • Type on your device to guess one letter or one word at a time
  • Correct letters will be revealed in their respective positions

Incorrect Guesses:

  • Be careful! Each incorrect guess adds a part to the hangman figure
  • The gallows, head, body, arms, and legs are drawn sequentially for each mistake

Winning and Losing:

  • Win by guessing the entire word before the hangman is complete
  • Lose if the hangman is fully drawn before you guess the word

Difficulty Levels:

  • This game has different difficulty levels. Choose one that suits your skill level
